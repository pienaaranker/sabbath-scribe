Key Features

Role Assignment

Efficiently assign church members to various roles like Preacher, Elder on Duty, and Sabbath School Hosts for each Sabbath service.

People Management

Comprehensive member database with contact information, role preferences, and availability tracking.

Schedule View

Interactive calendar view with filtering by role or person, plus powerful search capabilities for easy navigation.

AI Suggestions

Smart assignment recommendations based on member availability, assignment history, and role preferences.

Data Architecture

Person

  • id: Unique identifier
  • name: Full name
  • contactInfo: Phone/email
  • fillableRoleIds: Qualified roles
  • unavailableDates: Blackout dates

Role

  • id: Unique identifier
  • name: Role title
  • description: Role details

Assignment

  • id: Unique identifier
  • date: Sabbath date
  • roleId: Assigned role
  • personId: Assigned person

SabbathAssignment

  • roleId: Role identifier
  • roleName: Display name
  • person: Assigned member
