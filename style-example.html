Color Usage

  • Primary (#266867): Main brand color, headers, primary buttons
  • Secondary (#5d2030): Important highlights, secondary buttons, alerts
  • Accent (#b27850): Accents, tertiary elements, icons
  • Light (#e9ceb9): Backgrounds, borders, dividers
  • White (#fbfbfb): Page backgrounds, cards, text backgrounds

Typography

Headings

Heading 1 (Lora Bold)

Used for main page titles

Heading 2 (Lora Semibold)

Used for section titles

Heading 3 (Lora Medium)

Used for subsection titles

Heading 4 (Lora Medium)

Used for card titles and smaller sections

Body Text

Body Regular (Inter Regular) - The main text used throughout the application. It should be easily readable and have sufficient contrast with the background.

Small Text (Inter Regular) - Used for captions, helper text, and less important information.

Medium Text (Inter Medium) - Used for emphasis and important information.

Bold Text (Inter Bold) - Used for strong emphasis and very important information.
